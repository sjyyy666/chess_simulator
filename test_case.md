# Test Cases

## Basic movement
- Rook moves horizontally and vertically only
- Bishop moves diagonally and cannot jump over pieces
- Knight can jump over pieces
- Pawn moves forward and captures diagonally
- King movement and queen movement

## King safety
- King cannot move into check
- A piece cannot move if it exposes its own king
- Check is detected after a legal attacking move

## Special rules
- Kingside castling
- Queenside castling
- Pawn promotion
- En passant

## Game ending
- Checkmate
- Stalemate
